Privacy Policy

Last update: January 20, 2026

Thank you for using InventariosTA. Your privacy is fundamental to us. This policy details what information we handle, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Google Account Information: Your email address for authentication.
  • User Generated Data: Business profiles, products, inventory, sales, and contacts.
  • Synchronization Metadata: Device identifier for data integrity.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Main functionality and business management.
  • Secure authentication via Google.
  • Backup and synchronization through Google Drive.

We do not sell or share your personal or business data with third parties.
