General Functionality

Q: What is this application for?

A: It is a complete management system for your business. It allows you to keep a detailed control of inventory, products, sales, purchases, services, and customers.

Data Synchronization (Google Drive)

Q: How do I backup my information?

A: The app uses your Google Drive account to back up and synchronize your data safely.

Q: Is synchronization automatic?

A: Yes, you can activate the "Auto-Sync" option in the settings menu.
