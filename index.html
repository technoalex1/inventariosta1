🚀 InventariosTA — The control your business demands

Losing products, not knowing what sells most, or keeping inventory "by memory" costs money every day. InventariosTA exists to eliminate that chaos and turn it into control, clarity, and real growth.

It's not just another app. It's the tool used by owners who take their business seriously.

📦 Always Exact Inventories

Register products, categories, entries, and exits in seconds. Every movement is reflected in real-time, avoiding shortages, surpluses, and errors that affect your profits.

With InventariosTA, you know exactly what you have, what's missing, and what's moving.

👥 Owner Management Without Confusion

Shared inventory? Consignment products? InventariosTA allows managing inventories by owners, keeping each product clearly identified. Order, transparency, and total control from one single place.

📱 Multi-device: Your Business Doesn't Stop

Access your information from several Android devices at the same time. Ideal for teamwork, multiple points of sale, or remote supervision. Everything synchronized, everything updated.

📊 Sales and Reports That Reveal the Truth

Record sales easily and get clear reports that show what really matters:

  • Best-selling products
  • Daily movements
  • Business behavior

Smart decisions are born from real data, not assumptions.

🖨️ Sales Printing

Print sales receipts directly from the app. Improve customer experience and maintain professional physical control of every operation.

🧾 Fast and Professional Quotes

Create quotes quickly and orderly, selecting products and prices in seconds. Ideal for offering clear proposals to your customers before the sale, improving service, and increasing closing possibilities. Convert quotes into sales simply and efficiently.

💰 Automatic Physical Cash Drawer Opening

InventariosTA allows sending a signal through the printer to automatically open the physical cash drawer when making a sale. Ideal for points of sale, it speeds up customer service and improves workflow without manual handling or complications.

📁 CSV: Your Information Always with You

Import and export data via CSV files. Backups, analysis in Excel, or information migration without depending on third parties. Your business, your data.

📄 Export Your Products to PDF

Generate product listings in PDF ready for review, control, backup, or sharing. Professional, clear, and practical.

☁️ Backups That Give You Peace of Mind

All your information can be securely backed up in the cloud. Change phones, restore data, and continue working without losing anything.

🔐 Fast and Secure Access

Sign in with your Google account. No complicated passwords, no time wasted.
