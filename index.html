What is InventariosTA?

InventariosTA is a mobile application designed for efficient inventory management in small and medium-sized businesses. It allows you to register products, perform stock counts, manage entries and exits, and generate real-time reports. The app synchronizes your data securely with Google Drive for backups and restoration.

Main Features

  • Product and category management.
  • Inventory movement tracking.
  • Google Drive data synchronization and backup.
  • Detailed reports and statistics.
  • Intuitive and easy-to-use interface.

Why do we request access to your Google account?

The app uses Google Sign-In to authenticate users and Google Drive to store and restore inventory backups securely and privately.

We do not share your information with third parties.
