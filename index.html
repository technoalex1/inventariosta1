What is InventariosTA?

InventariosTA is a mobile application designed to efficiently manage inventories for small and medium-sized businesses. It allows you to register products, control stock movements, and generate real-time reports. Data is synchronized securely with Google Drive.

Main Features

  • Product and category management
  • Inventory movement tracking
  • Google Drive backup & sync
  • Reports and statistics
  • Simple and intuitive interface
