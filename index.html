Welcome to InventariosTA

The app to efficiently manage your inventory

What is InventariosTA?

InventariosTA is a mobile application designed for efficient inventory management in small and medium-sized businesses. It allows you to register products, track stock movements, and generate real-time reports. The app automatically syncs your data with Google Drive for secure backups and restoration.

Main Features

  • Product and category management.
  • Inventory movement tracking.
  • Google Drive data backup and synchronization.
  • Detailed inventory reports and statistics.
  • Simple and intuitive interface.

Why do we request access to your Google account?

The app uses Google Sign-In for authentication and Google Drive access to securely store and restore your inventory backups.

We do not share your information with third parties.
